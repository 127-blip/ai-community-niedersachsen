Who we are

AI for everyone in
Lower Saxony

AI Community Niedersachsen brings together people who are curious about artificial intelligence — from complete beginners to experienced engineers. We believe the best way to understand AI is to explore it together.

Based in Braunschweig, expanding across the region. Our events combine hands-on workshops, real-world talks, and genuine human connection — no hype, no jargon, just honest exploration of what AI can do.

The community runs in English and German, welcoming both locals and international members living in Lower Saxony.
